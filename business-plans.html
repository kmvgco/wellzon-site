Business Plans

Merchant Standard

The Merchant Standard plan is designed for solo practitioners (1 employee) who want to streamline their wellness business. Whether you're a massage therapist, esthetician, or nurse, this plan allows you to manage all your services through the WellzOn app. With a simple 10% revenue share on each service, you’ll gain access to our vast network of clients seeking immediate wellness care. As a Merchant Standard, you’ll enjoy complete control of your schedule, pricing, and services, all while keeping your overhead low. Join early and get $0 revenue share on your first three services, plus free setup of your business on WellzOn.

Merchant Plus

Merchant Plus is tailored for businesses with multiple employees and the need for more advanced management tools. In addition to accessing the WellzOn app, Merchant Plus users enjoy the benefits of the Merchant Portal, where you can manage staff roles, locations, services, and billing seamlessly. With a 15% revenue share per service, this plan is ideal for growing businesses looking to scale their wellness practice. Early adopters receive $0 revenue share on their first three services and a complimentary business setup on WellzOn, making it easy to get started and maximize your earnings.
