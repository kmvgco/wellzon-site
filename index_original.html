About Us

WellzOn is a wellness platform dedicated to making self-care accessible and convenient. We connect clients with trusted wellness and healthcare providers for immediate, same-day, or next-day services, either at home or in a nearby clinic. Our mobile app operates like an online marketplace, allowing clients to easily book services and merchants to manage their business seamlessly. With a focus on quality care and flexibility, WellzOn empowers providers and clients alike to achieve their wellness goals on their own terms. At WellzOn, we believe that wellness should be within reach—anytime, anywhere.
