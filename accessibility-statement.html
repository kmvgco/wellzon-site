WellzOn Digital Accessibility Statement

last updated 2024-10-01

Digital Accessibility Statement

At WellzOn, we are committed to accessibility, diversity, and inclusion. We are making ongoing efforts to have WellzOn’s digital properties be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability.

WellzOn has a goal to align with applicable accessibility standards, including those in the W3C’s Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standard and the EN 301 549.

Non-Accessible Content from Third-Party Sources

Please note, when you select a link from our web pages that leads to a third-party managed website (such as Stripe, PayPal, or Apple), platform, landing page, or subsite, you are subject to that third-party’s accessibility, privacy, and other site-specific policies and features. While we cannot guarantee that third-party websites will comply with the WCAG 2.1 AA, the EN 301 549, or other accessibility standards, we encourage our vendors to strive to offer accessible platforms and an inclusive experience.

Contact

We want to hear from you - If you would like further information about WellzOn’s accessibility efforts, have other accessibility questions or feedback, or encounter accessibility barriers on our digital properties, please contact us at support@wellzonapp.com.
